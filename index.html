Course Director

Dr Irfan Rind

Consultant Cardiologist
Wrexham Maelor Hospital

  • Course Director - Frontline Echo
  • Member, British Society of Echocardiography (BSE) Clinical Standards and Departmental Accreditation Committee
  • BSE Accredited Echocardiographer
  • Faculty Member, BCS Imaging Village
  • Specialist interest in Advanced Echocardiography, Stress Echocardiography, TOE and Cardiac CT

Why attend?

  • Intensive hands-on scanning sessions with real patients
  • Step-by-step image acquisition and optimisation
  • BSE Level 1 pathology and valve pathology teaching
  • Echo mannequin demonstration sessions
  • Practical advice on achieving BSE Level 1 Accreditation
  • Case-based sessions for frontline decision-making
  • Certificate of Attendance
  • Networking with experienced echocardiographers
