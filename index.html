About the course

Practical echocardiography skills for frontline clinicians

This intensive one-day course is designed for doctors and acute care clinicians who want to build confidence in basic echocardiography image acquisition, interpretation and frontline decision-making.

  • Real patient and mannequin-based practical teaching
  • Step-by-step echo views and image optimisation
  • BSE Level 1 pathology and valve pathology teaching
  • Small-group practical workshops and accreditation advice

Consultant Cardiologist, Wrexham Maelor Hospital

  • BSE Accredited Echocardiographer
  • Faculty Member, BCS Imaging Village
  • Specialist interest in Advanced Echocardiography, Stress Echocardiography, TOE and Cardiac CT
